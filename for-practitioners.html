Common questions from practitioners

Is RootWell & Star a diagnostic tool?
No. RootWell & Star does not diagnose, treat, or prescribe. It organises information, surfaces coherence patterns, and supports your existing clinical judgment. All decisions remain with you and your client.
Do I have to change my current tools?
Not at all. Early versions are designed to sit alongside what you already use – paper notes, EHR systems, or custom spreadsheets – and give you a cleaner, more intuitive view of the same reality.
How will pricing work for practitioners?
The current plan is a freemium base with practitioner tiers for deeper features: multi-client dashboards, protocol templates, white-labeled journeys, and access to aligned product partners. Early collaborators will receive preferred pricing.
What does “coherence” actually mean here?
In this context, coherence is a way of tracking how stable and life-giving a pattern is across time – emotionally, physiologically, and relationally. The Δ.72 Coherence Layer gives us language for that without collapsing into purely mechanical or purely mystical frames.
